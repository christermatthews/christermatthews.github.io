Christer Matthews is a public-safety executive with experience spanning municipal law enforcement command, homeland security, criminal investigations, emergency preparedness, organizational training, military security operations, and international advisory service.

His career includes leadership responsibility for patrol operations, specialized investigative units, homeland security and special events, crime prevention, public order operations, police training, firearms modernization, active-shooter preparedness, and regional public-safety coordination.

Before entering municipal law enforcement, he served as a United States Air Force officer. His assignments included command responsibility for personnel protecting strategic nuclear resources, management of security-forces training and logistics, and deployment to Iraq as a Military Transition Team advisor working with Iraqi Army Military Police.

His leadership approach emphasizes disciplined planning, accountable execution, personnel development, interagency cooperation, ethical decision-making, and continuous organizational improvement.
