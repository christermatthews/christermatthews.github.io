## Selected Career Achievements

### Public Order Operations

- Established the department’s Public Order Team and developed it from initial concept through operational implementation.
- Recruited and organized team leadership and approximately 35 volunteer officers.
- Implemented a recurring monthly training program based on National Tactical Officers Association guidance.
- Directed the transition to the Public Order Management Academy system as the department’s standardized public-order doctrine.
- Coordinated with neighboring police agencies to improve regional consistency in crowd-management training and tactics.
- Researched, selected, funded, procured, inventoried, and issued specialized public-order equipment.

### Active-Shooter Preparedness

- Directed department-wide ALERRT implementation and recurring active-shooter training.
- Planned and managed a multiagency active-shooter exercise involving police, fire, emergency management, communications, school personnel, community volunteers, and specialized response units.
- Developed scenarios, coordinated instructors and evaluators, managed logistics, and directed after-action reviews.
- Ensured all sworn personnel received required active-shooter training during each state training cycle.

### Training and Firearms Modernization

- Assumed command responsibility for departmental academy, incumbent training, recruit coordination, firearms qualifications, and training records.
- Chaired the department firearms committee.
- Directed research and recommendations for modernization of full-size and compact duty handguns.
- Secured executive approval for the department’s transition to updated optics-capable handgun platforms.
- Coordinated training demands across shared academy, firearms-range, and emergency-vehicle-operations facilities.

### Homeland Security and Community Partnerships

- Directed homeland-security, crime-prevention, and special-event functions.
- Hosted recurring security briefings for faith-based organizations and corporate security leaders.
- Provided tabletop exercises, civilian active-shooter response training, site-security surveys, and force-on-force training.
- Coordinated intelligence and information sharing with regional fusion-center and Joint Terrorism Task Force partners.
- Managed public-facing crime-prevention programs, volunteer initiatives, neighborhood watches, public presentations, and surveillance-camera trailer resources.

### Criminal Investigations

- Supervised specialized investigative units responsible for homicides, robberies, aggravated assaults, sex crimes, child-victim cases, family violence, and cold cases.
- Supervised or assisted investigations involving 23 fatal incidents and 31 deaths.
- Directed investigative review, case assignment, warrant approval, case filing, arrest planning, and after-hours response.
- Created a multijurisdictional investigative task force after identifying connected serial-murder cases across several North Texas cities.
- Supervised nationally significant capital-murder, officer-involved-shooting, serial-offender, and child-predator investigations.

### Military Leadership

- Led up to 96 Security Forces personnel responsible for protecting strategic nuclear resources.
- Directed continuous security operations involving access control, surveillance, alarm systems, patrols, armory functions, and response forces.
- Managed training, logistics, supply, safety, firearms support, and geographically separated armories for a security-forces organization of approximately 720 personnel.
- Supported firearms training requirements for a military installation population exceeding 11,000 personnel.
- Managed deployment-equipment readiness, warehouse operations, and multimillion-dollar resources.
- Served in Iraq as a Military Transition Team advisor supporting Iraqi Army Military Police operations.
- Managed a $1 million vehicle-maintenance contract and served as an accountable officer for operational funds and purchases.
